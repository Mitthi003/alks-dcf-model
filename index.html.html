Investment Thesis
Alkermes is a de-levered, cash-generative pure-play neuroscience platform with three durable commercial franchises (VIVITROL, ARISTADA, LYBALVI), a transformative narcolepsy pipeline (ALKS 2680 — FDA Breakthrough Therapy), and the Feb 2026 Avadel acquisition (LUMRYZ) that materially expands its sleep medicine franchise. Trading at <20× 2026E adj. earnings on a debt-adjusted basis, ALKS offers significant operating leverage as the pipeline matures.
